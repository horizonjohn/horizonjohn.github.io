Hi there! Thanks for visiting my page. 👋

🔭 I have a strong interdisciplinary background. I truly enjoy exploring the intersection of these fields, and my long-term goal is to develop and integrate various insights into surroundings to enhance our living efficiency and quality.

🔍 Master: My focus primarily revolved around leveraging AI technologies to assist designers in enhancing their design process:

  • Generative AI: Diffusion Models, GANs, AutoEncoder, Vision Transformers.
  • Efficient Learning: Self-Supervised Learning, Multi-Modal Learning.

🔍 Undergraduate: I gained extensive experience in various engineering projects with the following interconnected aspects:

  • Microcontroller Development: Jetson Nano, Raspberry Pi, Stm32, PLC.
  • Deep Learning: Image Classfication, Object Detection.
  • PCB Design: Altium Designer.
  • Mechanical Modeling and Analysis: SolidWorks, Ansys.

Starting in my freshman year, I joined the Dean’s research group and participated in three projects. The final project, for which I was responsible, achieved notable results. It led to a breakthrough in the textile field, passed a formal technical appraisal, and facilitated collaborations with several leading companies in the industry. Furthermore, I actively participated in various technological competitions and campus activities to enrich my academic experience and campus life as a whole.

Selected Publications

  1. AAAI’25
    ARNet: Self-Supervised FG-SBIR with Unified Sample Feature Alignment and Multi-Scale Token Recycling
    Jianan Jiang, Han Tang, Zhilin Jiang, Weiren Yu, and Di Wu
    In Proceedings of the AAAI Conference on Artificial Intelligence, 2025
    Abs DOI PDF Code

    Jianan Jiang, Di Wu, Hanhui Deng, Yidan Long, Wenyi Tang, and 5 more authors
    Proceedings of the ACM on Interactive, Mobile, Wearable and Ubiquitous Technologies, 2024
    Abs DOI PDF Video Code
